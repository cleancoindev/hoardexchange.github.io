Cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of text sent to your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the service or a third party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the service more useful to you. Cookies can be ‘persistent’ or ‘session’ cookies.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies to make our website usable and to analyze our traffic. You should also be aware that Hoard interacts in different ways with third parties such as Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit, Telegram, Youtube and Medium on our website and in our app. We have no control over the use of your data by these third parties. We are nevertheless working continually in order to comply with our responsibility as a joint controller, and to provide social media features.
